Joint Reaction Force/Torque

Validates VBD parent-joint reaction reporting for articulated rigid bodies. The pendulum cases compare the reported wrench against force balance, using R = ma - mg for the linear component and the corresponding torque about the child body COM. The video side plots show force residuals in N separately from torque residuals in N m, and the detailed page includes a separate soft-mode check.

Per-Joint Reaction Readout

Validates the VBD-only joint-indexed reaction readout on a closed four-bar linkage. The green right link has both an orange-green internal joint and a gray-green closure joint, so its green-link net joint load is the body-level sum while state.vbd.joint_reaction_f keeps the two joint loads separated. The detailed page also includes a standalone closed cable loop pulled apart at two highlighted cable segments.

Cable Tension

Validates cable stretch-tension access from simple equilibrium loads to pulley contact diagnostics. The hanging cases check T = mg and T = mg + F_applied; the pulley cases expose segment-wise tension gradients and a two-pulley capstan-style diagnostic under frictional contact.

Joint Friction

Validates per-DOF VBD joint friction for coast-down, static stick, sliding under applied drive, and kinetic-energy dissipation. The report compares revolute and prismatic velocities against the constant-friction analytic expectation away from the regularized zero-velocity transition.
