Joint Reaction Force/Torque

Validates VBD parent-joint reaction reporting for articulated rigid bodies. The pendulum cases compare the reported wrench against force balance, using R = ma - mg for the linear component and the corresponding torque about the child body COM. The video side plots show force residuals in N separately from torque residuals in N m, and the detailed page includes a separate soft-mode check.

Cable Tension

Validates cable stretch-tension access from simple equilibrium loads to pulley contact diagnostics. The hanging cases check T = mg and T = mg + F_applied; the pulley cases expose segment-wise tension gradients and a two-pulley capstan-style diagnostic under frictional contact.

Status

The pendulum and hanging-load cases are quantitative checks against analytic force balance. The wrapped pulley cases are diagnostics for the tension field in a compliant segmented cable: they are compared with capstan intuition, but not presented as exact ideal-string equalities. In the current results, the two-pulley middle-span tension is intentionally treated as qualitative because compliant contact reads higher than the ideal capstan reference.
Generated assets and raw validation data live next to each detailed page under reports/vbd_tension_reaction and reports/vbd_cable_tension.
