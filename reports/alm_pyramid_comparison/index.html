ALM Pyramid Contact Comparison

Six box-pyramid contact variants prepared for side-by-side visual comparison: new compliant ALM large-gap versus small-gap behavior, old hard AVBD, old soft contact, finite versus hard-limit stiffness, and ramped versus fixed hard-contact penalty.

Scene 136 rigid boxes
Budget 1 substep, 10 iterations
Contact gap 0.5 m baseline, 0.05 m in V4

Comparison Video

Six-way synchronized comparison assembled from the individual version clips. The block is centered and constrained to match the stack-stability report style.
