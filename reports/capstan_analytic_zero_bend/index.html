Analytical Capstan Sweep

Zero-bend sliding-string validation against the analytical capstan relation FL/F0 = exp(mu*pi).

Cable 96 segments
Reference theta = pi
Validation Sweep mu 0.0-0.5

Setup

The cable is pulled over a half-wrap cylinder with bend stiffness set to zero. The left side carries the known dead load F0; the right side is the measured load-cell side FL.

The reported force ratio is averaged over the plateau window after the cylinder has moved 10 to 40 mm.

Result

Hard contact tracks the analytic curve. Across mu=0.0 to 0.5, the hard-contact sweep stays within 1.72% of exp(mu*pi).
Soft contact is consistently high. At the default multi-mu budget, the soft sweep grows to +9.80% error at mu=0.5.
Solver budget mostly controls noise. More substeps reduce plateau oscillation, while hard contact already matches the force ratio closely at practical budgets.
