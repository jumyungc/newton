Rigid VBD Peaucellier-Lipkin Linkage Verification

Two planar straight-line mechanism checks for rigid-body VBD joint-loop closure. The reports track whether tracer point D follows the ideal Peaucellier line while revolute joint anchors stay coincident.

Examples2 drivers
Reference Budget5 iters, 1 substep
Primary Metricsline error, joint gap

Setup

Both cases use the same Peaucellier-Lipkin inversor geometry: fixed pivots O and P, equal links OA and OC, and rhombus ABCD. Gravity and contact are disabled so the measurement isolates joint closure and mechanism geometry.

Direct driver. A kinematic rotating link PB drives point B around the grounded pivot.
Slider driver. A kinematic slider drives a coupler-rocker pair, which then drives the Peaucellier cell.
Dependency axis. The report sweeps 2, 5, and 10 VBD iterations; substeps stay fixed at 1.
No soft/hard split. Contact and gravity are disabled, so the clean comparison is loop closure versus solver budget.
Slider convention. The slider guide's permitted rail displacement is not counted as a joint-anchor closure error.
Scale context. Peak errors are worst-frame samples. The direct 5-iteration peaks are below 0.3% of the 0.85 m main-link length.

Direct Driver

The direct case is the cleanest Peaucellier-Lipkin check: the crank PB is kinematic, so the report mainly measures how well the VBD revolute-loop constraints preserve the ideal inversor path.

5 iterations. Peak straight-line error: 2.01 mm; RMS: 0.304 mm.
5 iterations. Peak joint-anchor gap: 2.28 mm; RMS: 0.381 mm.
Iteration trend. At 10 iterations, peak line error is 1.07 mm and peak joint gap is 1.16 mm.

Slider Driver

The slider-driver case adds a prismatic guide and coupler-rocker driver before the same Peaucellier cell. It is useful because the driver itself is a constrained mechanism, not only a prescribed crank.

5 iterations. Peak straight-line error: 0.104 mm; RMS: 0.041 mm.
5 iterations. Peak revolute joint gap: 0.17 mm; RMS: 0.092 mm.
10 iterations. Peak revolute joint gap is 0.152 mm; the 5-iteration budget is already visually accurate.
