VBD Two-Heavy-Body Chain Joint-Contact Stress Test

High-mass-ratio capsule-link chain with two heavy spherical loads and a fixed capsule peg, testing coupled ball joints and rigid contact.

Chain100 links
Mass Ratio50000:1
Reference Iterations16

Setup

The chain starts straight and tangent above the fixed peg. A small initial velocity separates the load paths, then gravity and contact produce the wrap.

The report compares soft-soft, hard-hard, and hard-hard-history. The primary metrics are ball-joint anchor gap and post-solve normal contact residual.

Comparison Video

The video runs the validation scene at 16 VBD iterations and compares the three retained mode combinations side by side.
